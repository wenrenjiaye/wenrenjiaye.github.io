Research Projects

Member Rights Protection Mechanism in Mutual Insurance Organization
Supervisor: Prof. Wenjiong He, A.P. Hong Shi

Take surveys and cases analysis, excavated real examples about infringement of member rights in Chinese mutual insurance organizations.
Used Principal-Agent Theory and game theory to analyze existing problems, found problems of dual nature of contract, misdirection in advertising, principal – agent risk, made targeted recommendations.  

Driving Factor of Volunteer Service---under the Background of Pandemic
Supervisor: Prof. Qing Miao

Concluded volunteer group’s characteristics using data of volunteering activity and time during pandemic.
Proposed self-efficacy as a significant variable, used probit regression to analyze the relationship, stressed the importance of enhancing the positive feedback.  

How can Carbon Emission Trading Market Influence Residents' Income
Supervisor: A.P. Kai Fang

Analyzed absolute and relative change of residents’ income in surrounding areas, explored impact of carbon emission trading market on fortune creating and distributing.
Learned relevant research of spatial spillovers, took spatial econometrics as a tool to do empirical research.
