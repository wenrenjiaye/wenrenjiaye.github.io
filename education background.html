Education Background

Basic Information

I am a junior student jointly cultivated by Chu Kochen Honors College and School of Public Affairs Zhejiang University. I major in labor and social secure with GPA of 3.87/4.00, and minor in psychology. I am educated in the project of "Elite of Public Administration", which is composed of the top students from every faculty and held for first time, with high education aim. Thus, apart with major courses like public police and administration, I also learn the basic courses of politics, sociology, especially the economics courses, such as microeconomics, macroeconomics, econometrics, applied econometrics, public economics, labor economics and so on.

Disciplines of Study

Economics

I have received basic and standard economics training in the university. This kind of training teaches me to think in perspective of division and corporation, demand and supply, market and government, utility and cost. And those tools used in economics studying, like stata, enable me to do positive economics research.

Public Administration

Public administration is closely related with economics from the beginning. The common sense and public policy are stressed in Smith's THE WEALTH OF NATIONS, which are critically essential in my public administration learning.  I learn  major courses in social welfare  especially, they instruct me to know more about labor, family, vulnerable groups and so on. 

Psychology

Psychology, especially social psychology, help me understand how people think. It offers a lot of useful theories in micro studying. And I also learn some methods of experiment and related statistics knowledge, which are also essential in behavioral economics and experimental economics.

  2. Public Policy. Especially the issues about social governance, polycentric governance and charity.
