The effect of skipped generation parenting on children development

ABSTRACT: The aging phenomenon and the increase of work pressure provide a realistic basis for the general increase of skipped generation parenting in society. The influence of this way of rearing on children's growth is more and more worthy of attention. From the perspective of children's health, we use data from CFPS surveys from 2012 to 2018 to examine the effects of skipped generation parenting. In terms of children's nutrition, this parenting pattern will hurt children's development, especially parented at night rather than in morning. This effect can be moderated by the age, in which older children are less negatively affected, possibly due to the increase in self-care ability and school attendance. When the incidence and frequency of disease were examined, the basic conclusion was still robust, but the moderating effect of age was not obvious. We suggests that parents increase child care time, especially in the early years of a child's development.

Human Capital, Labor Market Distortion and Industrial Structure Upgrading

ABSTRACT: The transformation of industrial structure is related to the replacement of national leading industrial sectors and reflects the change of factor input. As an important factor of production, the free flow of labor plays an  fundamental role in realizing the transformation and upgrading of industrial structure. The structural distortion of the labor market will hinder this process. Based on the special background of China's urban-rural dual structure, we constructs indicators of the degree of industrial structure transformation and upgrading and labor market distortion, and uses the data of China's development from 1978 to 2019 to find the negative impact of the latter on the former. Take human capital in consideration, this effect can be weakened. Therefore, we concludes that in addition to policies such as reforming household registration system, focusing on narrowing the urban-rural education gap will also help upgrade the industrial structure by affecting labor market distortions.

Ethical Dilemma of  "Close City" in Pandemic -------from the Perspective of Rawls's Theory of Justice

ABSTRACT: The COVID-19 pandemic has affected every aspect of people's life. Administrators have the duty to safeguard the people's lives in such disasters. However, the crisis of disasters has narrowed the policy space for decision makers, and whether to close city has become a choice they must face. From the perspective of Rawls's theory of justice, we discuss the ethical dilemmas between freedom and security, economy and security behind the act of closing city, and distinguishes between general situation and Wuhan situation. It is found that the former situation is in accordance with the ethics of Rawls's theory of justice in the two dilemmas, while the latter is not. We also conduct a dialogue and comparison with utilitarianism, and finally put forward the understanding of ethical dilemmas generated in the process of research, these understandings and analysis can provide help and guidance for practice.

Tax Competition: Regional Competition under the Background of Fiscal Decentralization

ABSTRACT: Tax competition is the competition behavior that the local government carries out in order to attract capital and develop local economy after obtaining certain financial power. We review the first generation theory taking "charity government" as the core assumption and the second generation theory based on the perspective of public choice theory, provide the background and basis for the development of tax competition theory and model. We find the effect of transverse and vertical tax competition may change as the government efficiency index and political system change. The study of China elaborates the existence of horizontal tax competition, but the undetermined existence of vertical tax. We put forward some new directions for tax competition research in the end, and some new demands for the localization model and empirical research of tax competition under the background of tax reduction policy in China.

The History of Labor Dispatch in China

ABSTRACT: Labor dispatch has been introduced and led by the Chinese government since the 1970s. In the process of market-oriented reform and state-owned enterprise reform in the 1990s, it has solved a lot of work problems with its flexibility. In 2008, the "Labor Contract Law" was issued, which made the labor dispatch issue have real laws to follow. In 2012, the "Labor Contract Law" was revised to more strictly and specifically regulate the flooding labor dispatch market in China, and labor dispatch entered the standardized and controllable development stage. Complex tripartite relationship, the distribution of the employer liability, the secure for the economic interests and democratic rights of the dispatched workers , is always around the theme in the development of labor dispatching, and provides the direction for future research. With diversity of employment forms, researchers and legislators should think what kind of restriction accords with the situation in China.

The Risk of Corporation Pension and Management means

ABSTRACT:As an important part of the old-age insurance system, the corporation pension can alleviate the aging risks faced by the entire society at the macro level, and help individuals to spend their retirement life more securely at the micro level. We focus on the risks and impacts of the financial market, labor market, and global market that American corporation pension has faced in recent decades, especially since the 21st century. We study three widely adopted types of management means: risk transfer, risk control and risk avoidance. We find the lack of employee perspective and the lack of research on DC plan risk in the research process. Compared with American research, we put forward the future direction of Chinese research supplementing at the micro-qualitative level, creating characteristics in the growth of the second pillar, and setting new heights in the risk research perspective of the DC plan.
