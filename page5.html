ABOUT IT

Launched by the Lujiazui Street, Lujiazui Community Foundation, a local non-public foundation, was co-founded by the region's enthusiastic enterprises, the media and professional public interest personages on 25th, September of 2015. Deeply rooted in the community, the Foundation with international view can be able to promote the circulation of community resources, improve the well-being of residents and solve social problems through innovation. The Foundation focuses on the needs of community and firmly believes that a small group of the most determined people can change the world.

What did I do

Community Building

I participated in the projects of old community building, which was aimed at improving the living conditions of those old community.  And it was an important part of my work to organize residents like teenagers to take part in these projects, making self-governance possible and trying to avoid "Bowling Alone". 

"99" Giving Day

As a community foundation with property of charity, those funds of residents serves as a main source of each specific project. "99"  giving day held by Tencent offers a good platform for recruiting funds. During that time, I went to different communities to publicize our projects and recruit money. 

Project Application

In the foundation, I helped sort out and arrange the materials of projects, and made a solid as application to take part in projects selection in Shanghai. We finally won the prize, and I also write an article on WeChat official account. These materials also helped me accomplish research report The wandering of the subject status: The participation of social forces in the community building.
